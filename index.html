Professional Summary

I am a community organizer and program coordinator with 17+ years of experience managing teams and operations across youth development, conservation, and grassroots organizing. All of my work centers on supporting people and strengthening communities.

My background includes coordinating crisis response and youth workforce teams as Mayor's Crew Program Coordinator with WisCorps/AmeriCorps during the pandemic. Currently, I serve as Recreational Registrar for La Crescent Youth Soccer Association and Co-Owner of Heartland Solutions, a trail-building consultancy focused on sustainable outdoor access. I'm also launching Bluff City Soccer Club, a free youth soccer program addressing equity and access in sports.

As a founding member of Wisconsin Resist, I coordinate operations and network-building to strengthen mutual aid across the state. My creative practice includes concept development on projects like Fractal HR and the Sacred Pause app. I've worked in sales, business development, solar energy, conservation, and trauma-informed youth care. I use that breadth to connect people and resources, building programs that solve problems and serve communities.
